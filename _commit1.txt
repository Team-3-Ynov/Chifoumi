fix(proto): remove password_hash, add VerifyPassword and VerifySession (#180)

Remove password_hash field from UserRecordResponse (reserved field 4)
to stop the Argon2id hash from travelling over the internal gRPC wire.
Add rpc VerifyPassword to Users service so auth-service can delegate
credential verification without ever receiving the hash.
Add rpc VerifySession(jti, userId) to Auth service so the API JwtStrategy
can check revocation and user existence without re-verifying the RS256
signature already validated by Passport.
Add email to VerifyTokenResponse to fix the email regression in JwtStrategy.
